## What Users Are Actually Saying

The reviews are mixed — honestly, that's worth knowing upfront.

On the positive side, a number of long-term users highlight the value-for-money angle. One reviewer on Trustpilot mentioned using the service for over three years and finding the pricing affordable with solid performance compared to bigger providers. Others praise the live chat support for being quick and resolving issues fast — agents like Tabish and Trisha get mentioned by name in glowing reviews.

On the flip side, there are legitimate complaints floating around: a handful of users report slow provisioning times (sometimes taking days instead of the advertised instant setup), disputes over plan specs, and some frustration with the refund process — particularly when paying in crypto. The Sitejabber rating sits around 3.7/5 from 69 reviews, while Trustpilot shows a larger pool with a similarly polarized spread.

The honest read: 99RDP works well for a lot of people who want affordable admin RDP access for bots, automation, encoding, or remote work. If you need enterprise-grade SLAs and bulletproof support, they may not be that. For budget-conscious users who don't mind engaging with support when issues arise, the value is real.

They do offer a **48-hour money-back guarantee** on most plans (excluding residential plans) — so there's some safety net if it's your first time.
